
# Try TEPfactor for free during Dubai Fitness Challenge 2019

Published at: **2019-11-05T11:53:00+00:00**

Author: **Amy Mathieson**

Original: [Time Out Dubai](https://www.timeoutdubai.com/sport-wellbeing/sport/421923-try-tepfactor-for-free-during-dubai-fitness-challenge-2019)

If you’re fully immersed in Dubai Fitness Challenge 2019 and are fan of challenges, there’s another deal you’ll want to check out.TEFfactor Dubai is offering up 30 minutes of free fun at its JBR site until the end of the Dubai Fitness Challenge 2019 on Saturday November 16.If you’re up for the challenge, you can get involved with the adventure that will test everything from patience to fitness and logic.Head down to TEPfactor between 10.30am and 11.30am on weekends and 6.30pm and 7.30pm in the week and you’ll get half an hour of free play.There are three levels of difficulty – bronze, silver and gold – to make sure that everyone is pushed to their limits, and you can compete in teams of two up to six people.Every challenge has its own time limit and you’ll have to race against the clock, with each group taking part in mind-bending puzzles, balancing acts, tasks requiring patience, heroic feats and team challenges.And the good news is that, unlike escape rooms, you're never locked in – so you can try the challenges in different levels as many times as you like.To get the deal quote 'TEPDFC19' when booking or show the Dubai Fitness App when you get there.Are you ready to work out for free?Sun-Thu 6.30pm-7.30pm, Fri-Sat 10.30am-11.30am. Until Nov 16. The Walk, JBR www.tepfactor.ae
