Sanjay Kapoor's daughter Shanaya Kapoor has not yet made her debut in Bollywood, but she certainly has made a name for herself with her fashion outings and glamours social circle. The star kid often gets snapped hanging around with her BFFs Ananya Panday and Shah Rukh Khan's daughter Suhana Khan.
Recently, Shanaya celebrated her 20th birthday with her near and dear ones which included cousins Arjun Kapoor and Rhea Kapoor. Her Bff Ananya was also present at the venue for the cake cutting ceremony at her house. Shanaya looked pretty in a frilled off-shoulder crop top while Ananya was cool in casuals.
