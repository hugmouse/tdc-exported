The alarming air pollution in the capital of the nation has been the talk of the town for a while now. Delhi is currently going through a very bad condition as air pollution level has increased leading to citizens suffering from hazardous breathing problems and whatnot. Many B-town celebs are spreading awareness through their posts on social media. Joining the bandwagon is Priyanka Chopra.
PeeCee is currently in Delhi shooting for one of her upcoming projects opposite Rajkummar Rao. As she shoots in the capital of the our nation, she took to her Instagram handle and shared a selfie with a mask. She expressed how bad the situation has been in Delhi due to the rising air pollution and also prayed for the homeless who are helpless. She requested the Delhi people to take special care of themselves.
