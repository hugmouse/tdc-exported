
# Collier County middle school student charged with making threat at school

Published at: **2019-11-05T14:12:08+00:00**

Author: **WFTX Digital Team**

Collier County deputies say the 13-year-old girl is responsible for writing a threat on the school wall last week.
She is charged with written threats to kill or do bodily injury.
