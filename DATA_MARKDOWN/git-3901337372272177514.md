
# 'Messi & Ronaldo Are Monsters'

Published at: **2019-11-04T13:29:00+00:00**

Author: **Kurt Buckerfield**

Original: [Soccer Laduma](https://www.soccerladuma.co.za/news/articles/international/categories/messi-ronaldo-neymar-watch-1/lionel-messi-cristiano-ronaldo-are-monsters/666677)

Liverpool superstar Sadio Mane is among the best players in the world at the moment, but has played down talk of being on the same level as "monsters" Lionel Messi and Cristiano Ronaldo, whom he is up against for the 2019 Ballon d'Or.

The Senegalese winger was sublime for the Reds last season, but has seemingly taken his game to new heights in the 2019/20 campaign so far.
Mane's brilliance over the past year as earned him a place among the nominees for the Ballon d'Or, which he will challenge teammates Mohamed Salah and Virgil van Dijk for, as well as the two five-time winners.
Asked if he sees himself on a similar level to the FC Barcelona and Juventus forwards, the Senegal superstar insisted he is still working hard to match up to them.
"These players are monsters," he said, according to the Daily Star.
"I think that I am still far away from them.
"As I have said, I am going to do everything I can to come a little closer to them."
