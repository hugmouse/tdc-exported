
# DBS launches zero-cost business toolkit to attract high-growth startups

Published at: **2019-11-04T12:36:34+00:00**

Author: **Uma Devi**

Original: [The Edge Markets](https://www.theedgemarkets.com/article/dbs-launches-zerocost-business-toolkit-attract-highgrowth-startups)

SINGAPORE (Nov 4): DBS Bank has rolled out a zero-cost business toolkit that comprises a suite of banking and professional services for high-growth startups in the software, Internet and emerging technology industries.
According to DBS, the “DBS Startup Unleashed” toolkit comes on the back of venture investors putting some S$13.4 billion into Singapore-based startups over the course of the first nine months of the year.
