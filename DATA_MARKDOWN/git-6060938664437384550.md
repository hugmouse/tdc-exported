
# 'Pati Patni Aur Woh': Kartik Aaryan flaunts his Chintu Tyagi t-shirt at the airport

Published at: **2019-11-05T11:18:12+00:00**

Author: **TIMESOFINDIA.COM**

Original: [The Times of India](https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/entertainment/hindi/bollywood/news/pati-patni-aur-woh-kartik-aaryan-flaunts-his-chintu-tyagi-t-shirt-at-the-airport/articleshow/71923111.cms)

The heartthrob of Bollywood, Kartik Aaryan is all set to hit the screens once again this year with his upcoming comedy-drama 'Pati Patni Aur Woh.' The actor will be sharing the screen space with Bhumi Pednekar and Ananya Panday. The actor will be seen playing the role of Chintu Tyagi, who gets into a difficult spot as he tries to impress his wife Bhumi as well as his crush Ananya.
The trailer of the film was revealed yesterday and went viral instantly. The comedy-drama promises a laugh riot and Kartik Aaryan looks all set to win the hearts of the audience again. Just a while ago, the actor was papped at the airport flaunting his Chintu Tyagi t-shirt. Donning a pitch-black jacket over the white t-shirt, Kartik looked dapper as he stepped out.
Directed by Mudassar Aziz, 'Pati Patni Aur Woh' is the remake of the 1978 Hindi film of the same name which featured Sanjeev Kumar, Vidya Sinha and Ranjeeta Kaur in pivotal roles. The film is slated to hit the screens on December 6 this year. The film also stars Aparshakti Khurrana in a pivotal role.
