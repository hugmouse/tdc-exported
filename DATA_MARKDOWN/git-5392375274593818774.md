Xiaomi’s smart TV brand is doing quite well in the market. In fact, the company has a stronghold on the Chinese and Indian smart TV markets. The Chinese massive 11.11 sale started today and the Xiaomi TV brand took only 13 minutes and 42 seconds to reach 100,000 units. The massive discounts on different TVs contributed to the massive sales.
Xiaomi TV 11.11 sale discount starts with the Xiaomi TV 4S 75 inch which is selling for 4599 yuan ($650). In addition, the TV 4X 65 inches sells for 1999 yuan ($283) while the TV 4X 55 inches is selling for 1399 yuan ($198). Furthermore, the TV 43-inch is selling for 1099 yuan ($155) and the 32-inch version goes for 699 yuan ($99). The Xiaomi full-screen TV 55-inch and TV 4C 40-inch will be available for 1699 yuan ($240) and 999 yuan ($141) respectively from November 1.
You can find these deals on Xiaomi Mall, Xiaomi Youpin, Xiaomi Home, Jingdong, Tmall, Suning Tesco and other major e-commerce platforms. In addition, the general manager of the company’s TV Department revealed that Xiaomi’s full-screen TV Pro 43-inch has been certified, and it is expected to be available for sale in a week. It comes with a 1499 yuan ($212) price tag.
