BARABOO-Beverly Ann Hahn, age 78, of Baraboo, passed away on Saturday, Nov. 2, 2019, at Agrace HospiceCare.
She was born on Nov. 1, 1941, in Chicago, Ill., the daughter of Fredrick and Lillian (Dzur) Bourque.Beverly graduated from Taft High School in 1959. She was united in marriage to Tip Hahn on June 19, 1976, in Addison, Ill. Beverly was a Wisconsin Dell's business owner for many years.
