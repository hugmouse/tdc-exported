
# TNPL to take up Rs 2,520-crore capacity expansion programme

Published at: **2019-11-01T00:00:00+00:00**

Author: **PTI**

Original: [Moneycontrol](https://www.moneycontrol.com/news/business/tnpl-to-take-up-rs-2520-crore-capacity-expansion-programme-4596211.html)

Tamil Nadu Newsprint and Papers Ltd onÂ November 1 said it would undertake a capacity expansion programme at a cost of Rs 2,520 crore in two phases at its Tiruchirappalli unit.
Chief Minister K Palaniswami formally launched the plan by laying the foundation stone for it under the first phase costing Rs 1,100 crore to the Exchequer, an official press release said.
Under the first phase, 400 metric tonne of wood pulp- making unit, a chemical recovery boiler division and a 20MW generator would be set up, it said.
