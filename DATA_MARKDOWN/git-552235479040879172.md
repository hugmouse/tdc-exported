
# Barefoot man with walking stick hospitalised after being struck by car

Published at: **2019-11-04T21:09:23+00:00**

Author: **Douglas Barrie, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/douglas-barrie/**

Original: [dailyrecord](https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/news/scottish-news/barefoot-man-walking-stick-hospitalised-20812032)

A man with a walking stick and no shoes is in a serious condition in hospital after being hit by a car in Ayrshire.
The incident happened at about 6.20am on Monday on the B780 close to East Mains Farm.
He was taken to the Queen Elizabeth University Hospital in Glasgow and the road was closed for nearly six hours.
Police are still trying to identify the man.
Road Policing Sergeant Craig Beaver said: "Inquiries are ongoing to identify who the man is, and we believe him to be in his 30s.
