
# Adeel, Sameer star in MCCC’s sensational win over BOC

Published at: **2019-11-05T19:30:00+00:00**

Author: ****

Original: [Times of Oman](https://timesofoman.com/article/2178122/Sports/Cricket/Adeel-Sameer-star-in-MCCCs-sensational-win-over-BOC)

MUSCAT: Adeel Abbas picked up five wickets and Sameer Mohammed struck a match-winning 88 not out to power MCCC to a thrilling 2 wicket win over Bosher Olympic Centre (BOC) in a Junior League Under-19 clash at Oman Cricket’s grassy ground 2 in Amerat on Saturday.Batting first, BOC was dismissed for 213 in the 50 th over despite being comfortably placed at 102 for 2 in 25 overs. Introduction of Adeel started the slide as BOC kept losing wickets at regular intervals. Adeel bagged 5 for 38 while Sameer took 2 for 25. Rushil Dadwal (34) and Sanin Nisar Fahad (30) were BOC’s only notable scorers.MCCC was in deep trouble at 65 for 6 in the 21 st over before Sameer started to put his side back on track, putting on a game-turning 128-run partnership with Aaryan Nishit Punja (39 off 78) and then an unbroken 20-run stand with Mustafa (13 not out) to seal a sensational win for MCCC with only one ball to spare.Brief Scores (U19): BOC U-19 213 all out in 49.1 overs (Rushil Dadwal 34 – 5x4, Sanin Nisar Fahad 30 – 2x4, Adeel Abbas 5-38, Sameer Mohammed 2-25) lost to MCCC (U-19) 218 for 8 in 49.5 overs (Sameer Mohammed 88 – 10x4, 1x6, Aaryan Nishit Punja 39 – 3x4, Muzahir Raza 2-42, Ishan Kabir 2-11, Aman Mallya 2-19) by 2 wickets.
