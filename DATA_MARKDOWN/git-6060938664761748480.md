
# Aishwarya Rai Bachchan shares a picture with Aaradhya; also don't miss the caption on Abhishek Bachchan's t-shirt

Author: **TIMESOFINDIA.COM**

Original: [The Times of India](https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/entertainment/hindi/bollywood/news/aishwarya-rai-bachchan-shares-a-picture-with-aaradhya-also-dont-miss-the-caption-on-abhishek-bachchans-t-shirt/articleshow/71860392.cms)

Aishwarya Rai Bachchan and Abhishek Bachchan got married in the year 2007. The duo has been giving us major couple goals for a long time now. The couple along with their daughter Aaradhya are in Rome for work-cum-vacation. And hence they celebrated Aishwarya's 46th birthday in the capital of Italy. Earlier, the actress shared a picture of the trio in a single frame from her celebrations.
Now, she took to her Instagram to share another photo of them where we can see Ash and Aaradhya looking into the camera while Jr Bachchan is showing his back! However, you can't miss what's written on Abhishek's red outfit. It read, "forever". She captioned the post with a heart emoji.
