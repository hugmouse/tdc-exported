
# Photos: Kartik Aaryan unveils the cover page of Filmfare magazine's November issue

Published at: **2019-11-01T16:39:06+00:00**

Author: **TIMESOFINDIA.COM**

Original: [The Times of India](https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/entertainment/hindi/bollywood/news/photos-kartik-aaryan-unveils-the-cover-page-of-filmfare-magazines-november-issue/articleshow/71858228.cms)

Kartik Aaryan has impressed everyone with his acting skills and now he has several films lined up ahead. The actor is currently busy promoting his upcoming film 'Pati Patni Aur Woh' co-starring Ananya Panday and Bhumi Pednekar.
The actor often gets snapped on various occasions in the city. And today, he was clicked by the paparazzi as he unveiled the cover page of Filmfare magazine's November issue. For the event, he opted a green t-shirt with a brownish-grey jacket over it and black pants. He was all smiles for the cameras at the event.
On the other hand, the actor looked super cool in a printed shirt teamed with a matching pajama on the cover page of the magazine. He can be seen holding a glass of juice while in a bed and was all smiles for the photo.
