
# Honda Cars India sales dip 29% in October

Published at: **2019-11-01T00:00:00+00:00**

Author: **PTI**

Original: [Moneycontrol](https://www.moneycontrol.com/news/technology/auto/honda-cars-india-sales-dip-29-in-october-4595731.html)

Honda Cars India Ltd (HCIL) on Friday reported 29.44 per cent decline in domestic sales at 10,010 units in October as against 14,187 units in the same month last year.
The company also exported 633 units last month.
