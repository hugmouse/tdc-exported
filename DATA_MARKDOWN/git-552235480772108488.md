
# Body of missing mum tragically found two months after she vanished

Published at: **2019-11-07T11:55:14+00:00**

Author: **Nicholas Keyden, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/nicholas-keyden/**

Original: [dailyrecord](https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/news/scottish-news/body-missing-mum-tragically-found-20831826)

The body of a missing mum has been found in the River Spey almost two months after she vanished.
Jacqueline Ullmer , 61, was pulled from the water at Garmouth, Fochabers, on October 22 after she disappeared from her home in bad weathe r in Carrbridge, Cairngorms on August 31.
A major search was launched, with cops drafting in a Coastguard helicopter and mountain rescue crews to track her down.
Officers confirmed the grim discovery is Jacqueline and send their thanks to those involved in the effort.
Her family have been notified.
Detective inspector Donnie MacDonald said: "Our thoughts are with the family and friends of Jacqueline and we are supporting them through this difficult time.
"I'd like to take this opportunity to thank all those who've assisted us in this extensive inquiry."
