
# King Salman, Crown Prince Offer Condolences to Pakistani President on Victims of Train Fire

Published at: **2019-11-01T13:45:00+00:00**

Author: ****

Original: [Asharq AL-awsat](https://aawsat.com/english/home/article/1971821/king-salman-crown-prince-offer-condolences-pakistani-president-victims-train)

Custodian of the Two Holy Mosques King Salman bin Abdulaziz Al Saud and Prince Mohammed bin Salman bin Abdulaziz Al Saud, Crown Prince, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Defense have sent a cable of condolences on Friday to President Dr. Arif Alvi of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan on the victims of a fire that broke out on a train in Pakistan, the Saudi Press Agency reported. In this cable, the King said: "We have been informed about the news of the fire that broke out on one of the trains in the Islamic Republic of Pakistan, resulting in deaths and injuries. We call Allah the Almighty to bestow his mercy on the deceased, and wish the injured a speedy recovery."For his part, the Crown Prince said: "I have been informed about the news of the fire that broke out on one of the trains in the Islamic Republic of Pakistan, and the resulting deaths and injuries. I offer my condolences to Your Excellency and victims' families, calling Allah the Almighty to bestow his mercy on the deceased, wishing a speedy recovery to the injured ".
