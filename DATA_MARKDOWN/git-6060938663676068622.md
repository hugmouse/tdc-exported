Indian cricket team captain Virat Kohli is celebrating his birthday today and the skipper and his wife Anushka Sharma have jetted off to Bhutan for a much-needed getaway. Pictures from their trek in the beautiful mountains of Bhutan have been going viral on the internet. The duo also shared pictures and videos of them having a good time together.
Shrugging off their stardom, Virat and Anushka were seen having a pleasing time mingling with the locals and exploring new places. The couple event went on a trek to scale mountains. Both of them are travel enthusiasts and zeroed in on one of their favourite destinations to ring in Virat's birthday.
Just a while ago, Virat Kohli took to his social media handles to share a beautiful picture from their vacation. Enjoying the scenic view from the mountains, Virat and Anushka are looking very happy and content in this latest picture from their vacation.
